Combo Bounce House FAQ - Hutto TX

Common questions from Hutto families before booking a combo bounce house rental.

What is a combo bounce house rental in Hutto TX?
A combo bounce house is a single inflatable unit that combines a jumping chamber with a built-in slide - either a water slide or a dry slide. It gives you two activities in one footprint and is the most-booked party rental in Hutto TX and across Williamson County. The combo moonwalk is sometimes also called a combo jumper or slide combo.
How much does a combo bounce house rental cost in Hutto TX?
Combo bounce house rentals in Hutto TX range from $225 to $400 depending on size and whether the unit runs wet or dry. All prices include delivery, setup and teardown across Hutto and Williamson County. Contact us for current pricing on your event date.
Can combo bounce houses be used wet and dry in Hutto TX?
Yes - most of our combo bounce houses run either wet or dry depending on the season and your preference. Wet mode is popular from March through October in Hutto TX. Dry mode works year-round and is great for fall birthday parties and school events when parents prefer to keep things dry.
What size yard do I need for a combo bounce house in Hutto TX?
A standard combo bounce house needs roughly 15x20 feet of flat ground. Most backyard lots in Hutto TX 78634 can accommodate a combo unit on grass or concrete. Contact us with your yard size if you are unsure and we will recommend the right unit.
