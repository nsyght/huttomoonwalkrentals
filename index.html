What Is a Moonwalk Rental in Hutto, TX?

A moonwalk is the Texas-native term for an inflatable bounce house or bouncy castle. In Hutto TX - the Hippo Capital of Texas - and across Williamson County, locals call bounce houses moonwalks. The name comes from the weightless, floating sensation children feel while jumping inside the inflatable chamber. You will find the same spirit behind Hutto's famous hippo statues at every birthday party we deliver to: community, fun and a little bit of Texas personality.

What is the difference between a moonwalk and a bounce house?
They are identical equipment - different regional names. Moonwalk is the Texas term used across Hutto, Austin, San Antonio, Houston and Dallas. Bounce house is the national US standard. Bouncy castle is the British term. Jump house is common in Southern US markets. All describe the same airtight inflatable structure where children jump and play.
Can bounce houses handle Hutto summer heat?
Yes - with smart planning. Water slide rentals are the most popular Hutto summer choice for good reason. For dry bounce houses in summer, morning setups before peak afternoon heat work best. Units are shut down if conditions exceed safe operating levels or winds hit 15-20 mph.
