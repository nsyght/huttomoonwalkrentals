Who We Are

Hutto's Bounce House Rental Source

Hutto Moonwalk Rentals connects Hutto TX families with the best inflatable party rentals in Williamson County. Every moonwalk, bounce house, water slide and obstacle course booked through this site is provided and operated by Jump Around Party Rentals - a fully licensed and insured party rental company serving Hutto, Pflugerville, Round Rock, Georgetown, Taylor and the entire Greater Austin metro.

All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Williamson County and Travis County, Texas. Jump Around Party Rentals meets all Texas Department of Insurance requirements for Class B Amusement Ride operators, carrying a minimum of $1,000,000 commercial general liability coverage per occurrence and maintaining a current Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker on every unit.

Hutto is the Hippo Capital of Texas - and if you have driven through downtown Hutto you already know the famous hippo statues that put this city on the map. The same fast-growing, family-forward community that rallied around those hippos is exactly who we serve. New subdivisions are going up every year along FM 685, Limmer Loop, Chris Kelley Boulevard and Ed Schmidt Boulevard. Star Ranch, Emory Farms and Brookfield are packed with young families, and every summer that means packed birthday party calendars across 78634.

Whether it is a birthday party in a new subdivision, a Hutto ISD school carnival at Hutto High School, Hutto Middle School or one of the elementary campuses, a community event at Hutto Lake Park or Hutto Community Park, or an HOA block party near the Hutto Recreation Center - we deliver, set up and tear down so you can focus on the party. We also cover Taylor, Manor, Elgin and the full Williamson County corridor.
